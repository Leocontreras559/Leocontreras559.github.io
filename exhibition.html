Who is Leo Electric?

Born June 9th 2001, Leonard Luis Contreras also known as Leo Vestidos runs the house and is the founder of Leo Vestidos. For years Leo has created gourmets for both menswear and womenswear. Trying to push the boundaries of what we call clothes and breaking out of fashion trends by inventing new ones.
