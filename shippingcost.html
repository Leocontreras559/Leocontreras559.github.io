Shipping Cost

United States

Since we are manufactured and located in the United States shipping is the quickest it can be with around 2 to 5 weeks for shipping with a cost of around $5 depending on how much items purchased.

Mexico

If you are purchasing a item from Mexico be expected to receiver your item 3 to 7 weeks after payment of your item with a shipping cost of $10.

Italy

Our shipments for Italy can differ based on the season and holidays but shipping should start 3 to 8 weeks with a shipping cost of $10.

Japan

For our customers in Japan you should be expecting 2 to 6 weeks in advance with a shipping cost of $15.
